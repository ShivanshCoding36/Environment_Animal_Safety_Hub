Solutions & Prevention

Strategies to reduce bioaccumulation and protect ecosystems

Pollution Source Control

Implement stricter industrial emissions standards, mercury capture technology in coal plants, and better wastewater treatment

Chemical Bans & Regulation

Phase out persistent organic pollutants, implement REACH regulations in EU, and global treaties like Stockholm Convention

Consumption Guidelines

Develop fish consumption advisories, promote low-mercury seafood choices, and educate at-risk populations

Cleanup & Remediation

Dredge contaminated sediments, phytoremediation using plants to absorb toxins, and soil washing technologies

Reduce Your Toxic Footprint

Individual actions can help reduce the flow of toxins into our ecosystems

Choose Low-Mercury Fish

Select smaller fish like sardines, salmon, and avoid large predators like shark and swordfish

Avoid Plastic Packaging

Use reusable containers, avoid single-use plastics that break down into microplastics

Support Organic Farming

Choose organic produce to reduce pesticide runoff into waterways

Advocate for Regulations

Support policies that limit industrial emissions and chemical use
